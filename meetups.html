Radical Engineers health meetup:
Heal The World

Thursday, Feb 21 2019, 7pm - Newspeak House

Join us for our Radical Engineers meetup at Newspeak House!

This monthâ€™s theme is health. Whether you lead a healthy life still largely depends on where you are born, your socio-economic class, race, gender and sexual orientation. Given this reality, how do we ensure we all have access to health services? How do we ensure issues that affect the health of marginalised populations (e.g. drug addictions, or tropical diseases) get researched and treated? And, how do we promote healthy habits when our digital lives, increased social isolation and sedentary lifestyles are putting our (mental) well-being at risk? Many radical initiatives are working to tackle these problems. They need our help!

We are inviting organisations working in this area to meet engineers of all stripes (hackers, makers, designers and other technologists) who wish to contribute their spare time and talents to these projects. During the meetup we will run a speed dating session, for organisations to introduce themselves to our engineers.

Doors open at 19:00, event starts at 19:30

Radical Engineers meetup:
No Time to Waste

Thursday, Jan 24 2019, 7pm - Newspeak House

Join us for our Radical Engineers meetup at Newspeak House!

Our second meetup is themed around the idea of waste. From fast fashion to endless packaging, we are surrounded by the unnecessary. This meetup is about ways to get rid of it.

This could be dematerialisation, sharing, reuse and transformation.

We are inviting organisations working in this area to meet engineers of all stripes (hackers, makers, designers and other technologists) who wish to contribute their spare time and talents to these projects.

Location: Newspeak House, 133 Bethnal Green Rd, London E2 7DG
Date: Thursday, 24 January 2019

Doors open at 19:00, event starts at 19:30
